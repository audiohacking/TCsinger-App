## Audio Prompts

To use the demo effectively, you'll need audio prompts. These should be:

- 3-10 seconds long
- Clear recordings of singing or speaking
- Representative of the timbre you want to replicate

### Creating Your Own Audio Prompts

1. Record a short clip of singing or speaking
2. Export as WAV or MP3
3. Ensure good audio quality (no background noise)
4. Upload to the demo app

### Example Sources

You can use:
- Your own recordings
- Royalty-free singing samples
- Clips from public domain recordings

**Note**: Always ensure you have the right to use any audio samples, especially for commercial purposes.
